RCRI – Cardiac Risk Index

Goldman Cardiac Risk Index

Defined as one or more of the following:
  • PaO₂ < 60 mmHg
  • PaCO₂ > 50 mmHg
  • K⁺ < 3.0 mEq/L
  • HCO₃⁻ < 20 mEq/L
  • BUN > 50 mg/dL
  • Creatinine > 3 mg/dL
  • Abnormal AST
  • Chronic liver disease
  • Bedridden state
